
Here are the laws in one line each:

- **Murphy's Law** – "Anything that can go wrong will go wrong."
    
- **Kidlin's Law** – "If you can write the problem down clearly, you're halfway to solving it."
    
- **Gilbert's Law** – "The biggest problem with a job is that no one tells you what to do."
    
- **Walsen's Law** – "prioritizing intelligence and information will lead to financial success."
    
- **Falkland's Law** – "Don't make a decision when you don't need to"\
    
-  **Karma Law**: Your past actions determine who you are today  

- **Golden Rule**: Don't do to others what you don't want done to you
    
- **Kidlin's Law**: Clearly define your problems to make solutions easier
