	
**Product Manager (PM)** = Person responsible for guiding a product from **idea → launch → growth**.

**Key Points:**
- **Role:** Acts as the “CEO of the product” (but without direct authority).
- **Focus Areas:**
    - Understand customer needs
    - Define product vision & strategy
    - Prioritize features (roadmap, backlog)
    - Align teams (engineering, design, marketing, sales)
- **Skills Needed:** Business, tech understanding, communication, data-driven decision-making.
- **Goal:** Build the _right product_ that solves user problems and achieves business objectives.

---


**What is a product** : A good or service offered by a company which affords a bundles of benefits, both objective (Physical) and subjective (image), to a user

- it is a good or service
- it offers objective benefits 
- it offers subjective benifits

**Goal:** Deliver **benefits + satisfaction** to customers, not just features.

**Types of products :**
- Physical (phone, car)
- Digital (apps, software, websites)
- Service (ride-sharing, consulting)
