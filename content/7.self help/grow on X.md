X (Twitter) Growth Strategies


1. Use Threads with Images
Tweets that include images get better engagement.
Try creating visual threads to increase reach and retention.

2. Engage with Accounts Under 1K Followers
Interacting with small creators (<1,000 followers) helps build authentic connections.
They are more likely to engage back and support your content.

3. Avoid Using Too Many Hashtags
Hashtags reduce reach on X.
Instead, focus on clear and catchy text that drives interaction naturally.

4. Don’t Add Links in the Tweet Itself
Tweets with external links rarely go viral.
Instead, post the link as a comment under your main tweet.

5. Avoid Promotions When You Have Few Followers
If your account is still growing, avoid doing promotions early.
Focus first on building trust, engagement, and a loyal audience.
