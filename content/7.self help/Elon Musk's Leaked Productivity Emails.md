• **Meetings:** Avoid large or frequent meetings. Leave if you aren't adding value. 

• **Signal vs. Noise:** Focus 100% on actions (signal) moving you to your goal. Eliminate distractions (noise). 

• **Communication:** Use the shortest, most direct path. Avoid jargon and acronyms. 

• **Real Productivity:** Focus on what is productive, not what feels productive.

• **Common Sense:** Challenge rules that inhibit productivity. 

• **Environment:** Surround yourself with other productive people. 

• **Perfectionism:** Be obsessed with your work. Treat it like a craft to maintain focus. 

• **Ignore Distractions:** Focus on performance, not external noise like stock prices. 

• **Results Only:** The result is all that matters, not hours. Track measurable outputs. 

• **Be "Hardcore":** Be maniacally passionate and obsessed with your mission.
